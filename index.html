Welcome to Redfur Connect!

Redfur is a community joined together for the common purpose of ingame trade in The Elder Scrolls. During our initial push to open Redfur Trading it began to become clear that we were going to be something more than a bustling trade hub. As time went by our guild grew tremendously and we opened a PvP/PvE guild called Redfur Army and yet another trade guild called Redfur Exchange.

— Zlater
